Places of interest in Iduki

Idukki, a district in the Indian state of Kerala, captivates visitors with its breathtaking natural beauty and rich biodiversity. Home to verdant hills, lush forests, and meandering rivers, Idukki offers a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Travelers can immerse themselves in the charm of hill stations like Munnar, explore the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary, or marvel at the architectural marvels like the Idukki Arch Dam, India's tallest arch dam. With its scenic vistas and serene ambiance, Idukki beckons adventurers and nature lovers to embark on an unforgettable journey.
