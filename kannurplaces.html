Places of interest in Kannur

Kannur, situated in the picturesque state of Kerala, beckons with its pristine beaches, vibrant cultural heritage, and historic landmarks. Visitors can explore the tranquil shores of Payyambalam Beach or immerse themselves in the rich traditions of Theyyam, a mesmerizing ritualistic dance form unique to the region. With its array of ancient forts like St. Angelo's Fort and delicious local cuisine, Kannur offers an enchanting blend of relaxation and cultural immersion for travelers seeking an unforgettable experience.
