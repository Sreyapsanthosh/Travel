Places of interest in Kozhikod

Kozhikode, a coastal city in the south Indian state of Kerala, is renowned for its historic significance and scenic beauty. With its palm-fringed beaches, tranquil backwaters, and vibrant culture, Kozhikode offers a perfect blend of relaxation and exploration. Visitors can delve into its storied past by exploring ancient monuments, such as the iconic Mananchira Square and the historic Kappad Beach, where Vasco da Gama first set foot in India.
